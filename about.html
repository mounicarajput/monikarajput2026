Hi, I'm Monika

A builder, mentor, and advocate for Data and AI

My Mission

Over the past 6+ years, I've built AI products, mentored founders, and helped dozens of companies turn ambitious ideas into reality. But I've learned that technology is only half the equation—the other half is helping people understand it, use it, and grow with it.

My journey wasn't linear. Coming from a tier-3 college, I explored NLP, computer vision, and consulting. I faced career setbacks that forced me to reconsider my path. That pivot became my superpower. Today, I thrive at the intersection of AI, product growth, and developer advocacy—helping early-stage startups scale, founders understand AI, and developers build with confidence.

  • Worked with startups to scale from idea to product-market fit

  • Create content and resources to demystify AI for non-technical audiences

Speaking & Community

Developer Advocacy

My Journey

Why I Do This: Growing up and studying at a tier-3 college gave me unique perspective—it taught me to hustle, question assumptions, and find creative solutions. When I encountered setbacks in my early career, instead of giving up, I pivoted. Those difficult moments became the catalyst for embracing mentorship, speaking, and community building.

Today, I channel that same scrappy energy into helping others—especially those from non-traditional backgrounds—realize their potential in tech and AI. I want to show that your background doesn't define your future; your effort and mindset do.
